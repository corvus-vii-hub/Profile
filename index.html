FAQ & Contact

What do you draw?
I specialize in human designs. I am comfortable drawing almost anything, but please note I am not a professional industry artist. I do my best with every commission!
Do you draw Furry/Mecha?
Since I specialize in humans, complex furries or mecha might be outside my comfort zone, but you are welcome to ask! I can try simple accessories or hybrid characters.
What is your turnaround time?
Typically 1-2 weeks for sketches and 2-4 weeks for full illustrations. For comics, it depends on the length.
Do you draw NSFW?
I generally draw SFW content. Please DM me to discuss if your request involves mild gore or artistic nudity.
Payment Methods?
I accept GCash (for Philippines) and PayPal (International). Full payment is required upfront for small commissions.
